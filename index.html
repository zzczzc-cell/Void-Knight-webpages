Game Features

Enter a ruined underground kingdom

Void Knight focuses on exploration, precise combat, and environmental storytelling. Each area is designed to help players discover hidden paths, lost memories, and dangerous enemies.

Explore

Travel through forgotten tunnels, broken cities, and silent temples.

Fight

Use fast movement and careful timing to defeat mysterious creatures.

Discover

Find hidden stories through ruins, characters, and visual details.

About Team

A small studio building atmospheric worlds

Team Apple is a fictional indie game studio focused on dark fantasy environments, character-driven stories, and memorable exploration. The team creates visual worlds that feel mysterious, dangerous, and carefully crafted for players who enjoy discovery.

World Design

Designs ruined cities, underground paths, and environmental details that support exploration.

Character Art

Creates strange enemies, quiet characters, and a visual style that supports the game mood.

Game Feel

Focuses on movement, combat timing, and player feedback to make actions feel clear and precise.

Team Apple is a small indie game studio in Adelaide, South Australia.
